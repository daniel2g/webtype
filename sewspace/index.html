We’re a community of sewists, quilters, knitters, crocheters, rug makers, reusers, experimenters, teachers and students.

Sew Space welcomes people of all ages and skill levels to learn fiber crafts through our workshops and open studio hours.

Stop by our workroom!

Whether you want to mend your socks or work on an in-depth project, our experts and resources are here for you. No registration required; suggested donation $5.
